Common warning signs

Recognising these patterns is your first line of defence against scammers.

Red flags to watch for
  • Requests for immediate action or payment under pressure
  • Offers that sound too good to be true
  • Requests for personal or financial information via email/text
  • Poor grammar, spelling errors, or unusual phrasing
  • Suspicious links, unfamiliar domains, or hidden redirects
  • Urgency tactics — "Your account will be deleted in 24 hours"
