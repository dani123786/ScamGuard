General Safety Tips

Do's
  • Verify sender identity before responding
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Keep software and antivirus updated
  • Research before making online purchases
  • Trust your instincts
Don'ts
  • Never share passwords or PINs
  • Don't click suspicious links
  • Avoid sharing personal info with strangers
  • Don't send money to people you haven't met
  • Never pay upfront fees for prizes
  • Don't rush into decisions under pressure
