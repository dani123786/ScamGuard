If you've been scammed
  1. Stop all communication with the scammer immediately
  2. Contact your bank or credit card company
  3. Change all passwords for affected accounts
  4. Report to your local law enforcement
  5. File a report with the FTC at reportfraud.ftc.gov
  6. Monitor your accounts for suspicious activity
  7. Consider placing a fraud alert on your credit report
Quick protection tips
  • Never share personal info with unknown contacts
  • Verify all requests through official channels
  • Use strong, unique passwords everywhere
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Keep all software and apps updated
  • Trust your instincts — if it feels wrong, stop
