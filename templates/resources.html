What to Do If Scammed
  1. Stop all communication with the scammer
  2. Contact your bank or credit card company
  3. Change your passwords immediately
  4. Report to local law enforcement
  5. File a report with the FTC
  6. Monitor your accounts for suspicious activity
  7. Consider placing a fraud alert on your credit
Quick Tips
  • Never share personal information with unknown contacts
  • Verify requests through official channels
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Keep software updated
  • Trust your instincts
